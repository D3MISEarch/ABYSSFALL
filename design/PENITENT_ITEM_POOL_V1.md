# The Penitent — Starter Item Pool v1

Items should reinforce ritual setup, sigil geometry, chain reactions, and health sacrifice. Legendary items must change playstyle rather than only increasing numbers.

## Weapons

### Hook of the First Confessor — Magic
- +8% Ritual Blade damage
- Ritual Blade finishers grant +2 additional Fervor

### Bloodwriter — Rare
- +12% mark duration
- Heavy Ritual Blade attacks apply two partial Rite Marks

### Gospel of Iron Teeth — Legendary
- Martyr's Chain becomes a bladed chain that damages every enemy it crosses
- Pulling an enemy through a sigil instantly completes one partial mark
- Chain cooldown increased by 15%

## Masks

### Faceless Vow — Magic
- +8 maximum Fervor
- +5% movement speed while above 50 Fervor

### Mask of the False Saint — Epic
- Health-paid Sacrament costs 25% less health
- Healing received is reduced by 10% outside ritual activations

### The Witness Without Eyes — Legendary
- Fully marked enemies are visible through walls and darkness
- Activating a sigil behind or beneath a marked enemy deals 30% increased ritual damage
- Direct weapon damage is reduced by 12%

## Robes

### Ash-Sewn Vestments — Rare
- Standing in an active sigil grants 10% damage reduction
- Sigils persist 1 second longer

### Skin of the Unforgiven — Epic
- Taking a heavy hit creates a temporary defensive sigil under the Penitent
- 14-second cooldown

## Gloves

### Scripture-Stained Wraps — Magic
- +10% sigil placement speed
- Brand of Ruin costs 2 less Fervor

### Hands of the Red Scribe — Legendary
- Placing a ground sigil also paints a smaller linked seal beneath the nearest branded enemy
- Linked seals collapse together at 60% power
- Maximum active sigils reduced by one

## Boots

### Procession Greaves — Rare
- Ashen Procession travels 15% farther
- Completing its line grants a brief movement-speed burst

### Pilgrim's Broken Steps — Epic
- Dashing through a marked enemy leaves a stationary afterimage that completes one partial mark after 0.6 seconds

## Relics

### Reliquary of Nails — Rare
- Every fifth completed Rite launches a nail toward the nearest unmarked enemy and applies a partial mark

### Saint's Severed Tongue — Epic
- Chain-reaction kills grant 1 additional Fervor
- Ritual activations produce louder enemy-alert noise and may awaken nearby encounters early

### The Last Confession — Legendary
- Brand of Ruin can be placed on two enemies simultaneously
- Damage echoing between the two primary brands is increased
- If either branded target dies, the surviving brand becomes unstable and detonates after 2 seconds

### Heart of the Apostate — Mythic
- Fervor no longer decays outside combat
- At 100 Fervor, health slowly drains until a major ritual is activated
- Revelation grants lifesteal and briefly changes red sigils to neon green

## Consumables

### Vial of Consecrated Blood
- Instantly grants 30 Fervor
- Costs 8% current health

### Black-Wax Seal
- The next ground sigil costs no Fervor and lasts 50% longer

### Ash of a Nameless Martyr
- Revives the Penitent at 25% health after a fatal hit
- All Fervor is lost and every active sigil collapses harmlessly

## Drop philosophy

- Common and Magic items improve reliability.
- Rare items strengthen one part of the core loop.
- Epic items introduce a meaningful advantage with a modest condition or drawback.
- Legendary items alter ability behavior.
- Mythic items reshape the class resource economy and should be extremely rare.
- The Penitent and Void Warlock may share neutral armor or relic drops, but class-defining items should never dilute their identities.
