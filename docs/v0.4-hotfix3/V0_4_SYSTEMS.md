# v0.4 Systems Reference

## Room state flow

`Courtyard → Generator Chamber → Catacombs Wave 1 → Catacombs Wave 2 → Trap Hall → Hollow King`

Each combat room locks the next gate until its generator or enemies are cleared. Entering the next section starts that encounter, keeping unused rooms quiet and reducing unnecessary active enemies.

## New enemy roles

### Bone Archer

- Maintains distance from the Warlock
- Retreats when crowded
- Fires dodgeable purple projectiles
- Low health; ideal Rift target

### Crypt Brute

- High health and slow movement
- Heavy melee attack
- Periodic close-range ground slam
- Reduced displacement from Grasping Rift

## Hidden relic

The trap-hall side alcove contains the **Reliquary of Sunken Teeth**. Approach it and press **E / B**. It grants Life, Corruption capacity, soul healing, and bonus XP.

## Hollow King

### Phase 1 — The Preacher

- Three-bolt fan
- Reaver summons
- Controls medium range

### Phase 2 — The Corruptor

- Five-bolt fan
- Radial corruption nova
- Adds Bone Archers
- Increased movement and melee pressure

### Phase 3 — The Hollow King

- Seven-bolt fan
- Larger and faster novas
- Adds a Crypt Brute
- Void-steps around the player
- Fast melee pressure

Defeating him awards the **Crown of the Hollow King**, a Legendary hood that increases maximum Corruption, Corruption gain, and Rift damage.

## Loot expansion

The pool now covers multiple options for all six equipment slots. Item stats continue to stack with the eighteen-node skill tree, allowing hybrid builds rather than replacing level-up choices.
