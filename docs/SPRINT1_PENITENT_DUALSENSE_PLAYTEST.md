# Sprint 1 — Penitent Combat and DualSense Playtest

This focused pass follows the first Windows graphical playtest.

## DualSense controls

- Left stick: move and set Ritual Blade facing
- R1: Ritual Blade
- Cross: dodge
- L1: Seal of Binding
- R3: Brand of Ruin
- L3: Martyr's Chain
- D-pad Down: Sacrament
- Triangle: inventory
- Square: skill tree
- Circle: interact

The bottom control strip should switch between keyboard/mouse, PlayStation, and Xbox wording after the latest input from that device family.

## Ritual Blade checks

1. Stand roughly three character widths from the first pack and press R1.
2. Confirm the Penitent performs a short controlled step-in only when a target is in the frontal arc.
3. Confirm a wide glowing slash arc appears even when the attack misses.
4. Confirm enemies in front and slightly to either side can be hit without body-stacking them.
5. Confirm enemies behind the Penitent are not hit.
6. Confirm the three attacks remain 10, 12, and 18 damage and the third strike reads as the finisher.
7. Confirm the step-in does not push through walls or enemies.

## Regression checks

- Void Warlock movement, range, and attacks should feel unchanged.
- Penitent dodge and all five active skills should still respond.
- Camera follow and enemy spawning should remain as smooth as the first playtest.
- Report any debugger errors, stuck movement, controller disconnects, or misleading prompts.
