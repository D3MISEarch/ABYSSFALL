# 1. Core game promise

**AbyssFall is a brutal dark-fantasy action RPG where damaged survivors weaponize the incompatible forces holding reality together. It combines savage, heavy-metal combat with oppressive mythic storytelling, persistent character progression, deep buildcraft, and a world whose salvation may be more horrifying than its collapse.**

The game must feel:

- brutal;
- dreadful;
- mythic;
- heavy;
- mechanically deep;
- initially understandable;
- cinematic without becoming passive;
- unmistakably AbyssFall.

---

# 2. Creative north star

AbyssFall pursues four quality targets:

## 2.1 Physical brutality

Combat must communicate force through:

- heavy hit reaction;
- stagger and posture damage;
- execution windows;
- blood, debris, broken armor, torn tissue, warped environments, and displaced bodies;
- strong animation anticipation and follow-through;
- sound that gives attacks physical mass;
- readable violence rather than decorative particle clutter.

The brutality should feel aggressive and empowering while remaining appropriate to an isometric action RPG. It must not become weightless spectacle, comedy gore, or constant visual noise.

## 2.2 Heavy soundtrack identity

The soundtrack should combine:

- crushing modern metal;
- mechanical or industrial pressure;
- ritual ambience;
- hostile low-frequency sound design;
- catastrophic percussion;
- selective choir, synthetic texture, or dissonant orchestration;
- dynamic escalation between exploration, threat, elite combat, boss phases, and aftermath.

Music should make combat hit harder and the world feel larger. Silence, ambience, and restrained tension remain necessary so the heaviest moments retain impact.

## 2.3 Dark campaign storytelling

The campaign must deliver:

- tragedy with consequence;
- memorable characters with goals beyond lore exposition;
- oppressive regions shaped by history and function;
- environmental mythology that supports an understandable main plot;
- mysterious forces without withholding every meaningful answer;
- victories that stabilize, reclaim, expose, or delay catastrophe rather than erase it;
- costly, local, earned hope.

## 2.4 Deep action-RPG play

The game requires:

- responsive real-time combat;
- meaningful loot;
- persistent characters;
- persistent equipment and class-specific progression;
- multiple saved builds;
- build-changing unique items;
- broad endgame build diversity;
- boss-safe class mechanics;
- mastery through synergy, sequencing, positioning, and build knowledge;
- no mandatory in-combat three-choice upgrade interruption;
- no seasonal character resets.

Reference games define ambition and quality only. AbyssFall must not copy another game’s cosmology, classes, passive tree, economy, combat structure, visual motifs, dialogue, bosses, map progression, factions, or plot reveals.

# 3. Shared-world and campaign law

AbyssFall uses:

- one shared world;
- one shared campaign timeline;
- one escalating central crisis;
- personal class arcs integrated into that campaign.

No launch class receives a separate universe or separate campaign.

Class identity may alter:

- dialogue;
- optional encounters;
- personal missions;
- class revelations;
- progression trials;
- selected quest outcomes;
- ending context.

These differences must not multiply the complete campaign into eight separately produced campaigns.

# 4. Reconciled cosmology foundation

## 4.1 World-Lattice

The **World-Lattice** is the broad family of incompatible containment systems used across history to force a wounded reality to remain coherent.

It is not one clean machine built by one culture. It is layered emergency infrastructure built, inherited, repaired, exploited, and partly forgotten across multiple civilizations.

Its anchoring principles include spatial reference, ritual law, adaptive biological repair, dream-law containment, reliquary intelligence, fixation and mineralized stasis, impossible pressure, and temporal restraint.

## 4.2 Black Measure and the Last Measure

The **Black Measure** remains the approved spatial truth of rejected positions, distances never traveled, unresolved locations, and the impossible Mass of places reality refused to occupy.

The **Last Measure** remains the specialist engineering order that discovered impossible Mass and developed Manifold technology. It did not create the entire World-Lattice. It inherited, rediscovered, repaired, and connected to an older global containment structure.

## 4.3 Meridian system

- **Meridian Vaults:** spatial-reference facilities positioned around major fault lines and Black Measure pressure.
- **Axis Vault:** the Last Measure’s attempt to establish one universal coordinate.
- **Integrated Manifold Harness:** living operator interface that lets a Voidbringer act as a Reference Body.
- **Mass Anchors, Fold Lines, Instability, Breach, and Clean Closure:** protected Voidbringer concepts and vocabulary; never generic gravity magic.
- **Manifold Trials:** class-specific Voidbringer progression and narrative content reached through optional spaces integrated into the shared campaign.

## 4.4 Edras Vey and the Architect Below

**Edras Vey** remains the complete Voidbringer personal antagonist and creator of the Stillpoint doctrine. His plan exploits the world’s spatial failure and attempts to end separation by collapsing all existence into one absolute location.

The **Architect Below** remains a larger unresolved cosmological question and is deliberately ambiguous through launch.

Edras Vey is not the Architect Below. Defeating Edras must not automatically explain or resolve the deeper Abyss.

# 5. Campaign architecture

The intended complete campaign vision contains six acts:

1. **Sunken Crypts and the Hollow King**
2. **Red Basilica**
3. **Country Behind Sleep**
4. **Reliquary March**
5. **Inverted Sea**
6. **Hour Without End**

These acts reveal one worsening truth:

- the world is structurally wounded;
- stability was maintained through sanctioned cruelty;
- identity and perception are containment components;
- memory and the dead are active infrastructure;
- pressure systems contain non-human histories;
- time and causality were also bound into the failing architecture.

The six acts are not one simultaneous first production milestone.

## 5.1 Milestone A — Act 01 vertical campaign

Milestone A must prove:

- exploration and quest flow;
- one refuge or settlement;
- one hostile region with branching routes;
- elites and one dungeon arc;
- one major boss;
- persistent loot, leveling, equipment, saved builds, and save continuity;
- environmental storytelling and cinematic tone without excessive cutscene dependence;
- replay value through builds and optional encounters;
- one integrated class-personal mission.

The first class-personal mission integration uses **Penitent**, because Penitent is currently the most production-ready playable class.

Voidbringer follows as the deeper flagship campaign integration after the shared Act 01 structure and current runtime loop are proven together.

## 5.2 Later milestones

- **Milestone B:** expand to a coherent two- or three-act early-campaign arc after measured Act 01 production data exists.
- **Milestone C:** complete all six acts as one escalating descent rather than unrelated biome demonstrations.

# 8. Revised, deferred, and held class boundaries

## 8.1 Somnarch — revised pending prototype

Not implementation-approved until a territory-readability prototype proves arena flexibility, telegraph visibility, controller clarity, beginner accessibility, and boss-safe Nightmare Pressure.

## 8.2 Relic Host — revised pending prototype

Not implementation-approved until a combat-tempo prototype proves that Council setup is long-term buildcraft, Presence rotation is fast and tactile, Memory Conditions reward active play, and possession never becomes menu administration, passive aura stacking, or loss of agency.

## 8.3 Tidewrought — revised pending prototype

Not implementation-approved until an arena-geometry prototype proves Current Lines function across varied spaces, forced movement preserves telegraphs, Pressure Fracture substitutes against resistant bosses, and pressure remains distinct from Voidbringer gravity.

## 8.4 Anachron — held

Anachron remains a preserved design candidate but is held from production implementation pending a deterministic replay spike.

The spike must prove exactly-once damage, cost, cooldown, proc, death, and reward behavior; collision-safe movement; target validity; deterministic results; session isolation; and no restoration of destroyed world or boss state.

# 9. Shared progression decisions

## 9.1 Respec

Approved direction:

- multiple saved builds;
- low-cost common-currency respec;
- free or nearly free early experimentation;
- understandable low endgame cost;
- no bespoke respec currency at this stage;
- no respec during combat;
- no repetitive farming merely to correct or test a build.

## 9.2 Attributes — deferred

`Might / Finesse / Will / Vitality` remains placeholder language.

No final attribute model, naming, scaling, equipment requirements, or character-sheet layout may be locked before itemization prototyping proves the model across multiple class fantasies.

## 9.3 Class-item slot — deferred

No universal or bespoke class-item-slot model is approved.

The Graftborn vertical slice remains slot-agnostic and must test identity, UI, loot-table, save, and fairness costs before a global decision.

# 10. Beginner-accessibility law

AbyssFall will not add a generic class merely to imitate another launch roster.

Every class must provide:

- a one-sentence beginner promise;
- a simple starter loop;
- gradual introduction of secondary systems;
- one viable starter build without external research;
- readable boss interaction;
- advanced mastery through sequencing, positioning, synergy, and build knowledge.

Complexity is allowed. Immediate administrative overload is not.
